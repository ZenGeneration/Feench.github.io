3. Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions:

  • Q: How do I restore my subscription?
    A: Go to the app's subscription section and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • Q: Does Feench collect any personal information?
    A: No, Feench does not collect any personal information.
  • Q: Can I use the app offline?
    A: Yes, Feench works fully offline except for subscription verification.
